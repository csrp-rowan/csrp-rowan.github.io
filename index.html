About

The CS Research Presentation is the go-to place to expose yourself to the latest research in different areas of Computer Science. Every week, a student or faculty member will be presenting recently published top research, followed by a discussion. After each presentation, there will be time to socialize. Light snacks will be provided.

The CS Weekly Research Presentation is a great opportunity to learn about and discuss recent research in Computer Science, socialize with research faculty and students, learn about paid research opportunities at CS Department, have an opportunity to add a valuable item in your CV/Resume, come up with new research ideas, establish research collaborations, become a better researcher and presenter, and have a lot of fun.

FAQ

Who can attend?

Anyone with an interest in Computer Science is welcome to join.

Do I need to register?

No.

How to sign up to present?

Most sign-ups have already been completed. If you see an empty spot in the schedule, contact Dr. Ivanov to sign up for presentation.

Will there be a presentation this week?

Each week's presentation will be announced via e-mail to all students, faculty, and staff of the Computer Science Department. All possible cancellations will also be announced. Also, the schedule below is timely updated. If you are not from the Computer Science department, please refer to the schedule below or send an e-mail to Dr. Ivanov to confirm.
