About

The CS Research Presentation is the go-to place to expose yourself to the latest research in different areas of Computer Science. Every week, a student or faculty member will be presenting recently published top research, followed by a discussion. After each presentation, there will be time to socialize.

The CS Weekly Research Presentation is a great opportunity to learn about and discuss recent research in Computer Science, socialize with research faculty and students, learn about paid research opportunities at CS Department, have an opportunity to add a valuable item in your CV/Resume, come up with new research ideas, establish research collaborations, become a better researcher and presenter, and have a lot of fun.

Time & Location

When: Fridays at 2:00-3:00 PM January 31 - April 25, 2025 (except Spring Break)
